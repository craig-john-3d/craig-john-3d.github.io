About
Craig John is a fourth-year student at Pennsylvania State University, pursuing a B. Des in Digital Art and Media Design, with an expected graduation date in May 2024. As a digital artist, he specializes in computer-generated projects, including films and online web experiences. Proficient in various tools like Blender, the Adobe Suite, and coding languages like Java, JavaScript, and Python, Craig brings his creations to life through photorealistic landscapes and interactive designs. Alongside his academic pursuits, he actively engages in extracurricular activities, such as the Digital Media Group and contributing as a graphic designer for Onward State. Looking ahead, Craig aims to leverage his skills as a 3D artist in architecture, film, or advertising, while also creating captivating and immersive online experiences through web design and 3D expertise.
